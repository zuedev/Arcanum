🔒 PRIVACY POLICY 🔒

📝 The Simple Truth

We believe in keeping things simple and honest. Here's exactly what we collect and why:

🗃️ What We Store

  • Discord Channel ID: To keep your tracker items organized per channel
  • Item Names: Whatever you add to your tracker (converted to lowercase)
  • Item Quantities: How many of each item you have
  • Timestamps: When items were created or last updated

That's it! No personal information, no message content, no user profiles.

🚫 What We DON'T Store

  • ❌ Your messages or chat history
  • ❌ Your Discord username or profile
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information
  • ❌ Voice data or media files
  • ❌ Server information or member lists
  • ❌ Any data outside of what you explicitly save with commands

🛡️ How We Protect Your Data

  • 🔐 Secure MongoDB database with encryption
  • 🔒 No data sharing with third parties
  • 🧹 Automatic cleanup when items reach zero quantity
  • 🏠 Data stays within your Discord channels
  • 👮 Only channel managers can clear tracker data

🗑️ Data Deletion

Want your data gone? Easy!

  • Use /tracker clear to remove all items from a channel
  • Remove the bot from your server to stop new data collection
  • Contact us on GitHub for complete data deletion

📞 Questions?

This bot is 100% open source! You can review the code yourself at GitHub.

Have concerns? Create an issue on our GitHub repository and we'll address it!
